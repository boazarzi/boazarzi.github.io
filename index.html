About Guru Apps

Guru Apps is on a mission to make every restaurant visit effortless. We started with Menu Guru and Event Guru—two AI‐powered tools designed to streamline your dining and event planning. As your “restaurant companion,” we’ll continue rolling out new features to cover reservations, reviews, and more.

Our team believes that technology should enhance real‐world experiences, not get in the way. That’s why each Guru Apps product lives on your phone, ready to serve you every time you step into a restaurant.
