Our Products

Event Guru

Turn any event flyer into your next calendar event in seconds. Event Guru lets you capture any flyer or poster—either live with your phone’s camera or by uploading a saved image—and automatically extracts the date, time, location, and details using OCR. In just a few taps, it creates a calendar event for you, so you never miss what’s coming up.

Open Event Guru on your mobile device.

Menu Guru

Understand the menu, see allergens, dine confidently. Menu Guru lets you snap a photo of any restaurant menu—no matter the language—or upload a saved image, and instantly analyzes each dish to highlight allergens and provide clear descriptions in your native tongue. Say goodbye to guessing ingredients or worrying about allergies; Menu Guru turns any menu into a personalized guide for confident, informed dining.

Open Menu Guru on your mobile device.

Cycle Goddess

Effortlessly sync your cycle predictions to your Google Calendar. Cycle Goddess empowers you to take command of your menstrual health with effortless precision. Simply tap to log the first day of your period or mark ovulation, and the app will calculate and display your upcoming cycle and fertile window for the next six months. Unlike other trackers, Cycle Goddess seamlessly integrates with your Google Calendar—creating all-day events and customizable reminders for period starts and ovulation days—so you never miss a beat. Your privacy is paramount: Cycle Goddess never reads your existing calendar entries, its developers cannot access your cycle data, and no information is ever shared with third parties. With its unique calendar‐syncing capability and iron-clad privacy, Cycle Goddess is the only companion you need to stay in tune with your body’s rhythm..
